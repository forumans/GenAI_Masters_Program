Problem Statement: 
In today's data-driven world, organizations are accumulating vast amounts of data from 
various sources. However, extracting meaningful insights from this data and turning them 
into actionable strategies can be challenging. Businesses often struggle to make data
driven decisions due to a lack of effective visualization and analysis tools that enable easy 
exploration and interpretation of complex data sets. 
The "Data Insights Dashboard" aims to address this problem by providing an end-to-end 
solution for data visualization and analysis. The dashboard will empower businesses to 
unlock actionable business intelligence from their data, enabling them to make informed 
decisions and drive growth. 

Description: 
Food delivery services are highly prevalent in metropolitan areas, where there is a high 
concentration of delivery drivers. These drivers receive order requests from customers, 
and once they accept the request, the process of delivering the food begins, ending with 
the customer receiving their order. After the completion of the order, customers have the 
opportunity to provide ratings, which play a significant role in determining their 
satisfaction. Ratings are given for both the delivery service and the quality of the food. 
 
Dataset: 
“onlinedeliverydata.csv” this dataset was collected from the residents of Bangalore 
which studies on factors which are contributing to the demand of food delivery in the city. 
 
The dataset has nearly 55 variables based on the following titles: 
 
1. Demographics of consumers 
2. Overall/general purchase decision 
3. Time of delivery influencing the purchase decision 
4. Rating of Restaurant influencing the purchase decision

Objective:  
1. Understand the Data 
2. Perform Exploratory data analysis  
3. Provide Data Insights 
a. Delivery Time Analysis: 
i. Minimum Delivery time 
ii. Maximum Delivery time 
iii. Find out the locality with most number of orders 
iv. Find out the locality with least number of orders 
b. Customer Satisfaction and Reviews 
c. Customer Preference: 
i. Top 5 restaurants 
ii. Top 5 Cuisines ordered
